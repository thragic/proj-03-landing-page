D A L A B É L


est.2020


Sustainable Handmade in England

Dalabél is a small independent British slow fashion brand, founded in May 2020 by a UCA graduate in Fashion Atelier. The brand itself is inspired by vintage silhouette where harmonious sensibility of artistic feminity details in exceptional quality.

ONE-OF-A-KIND

This collection consists of up-cycled ONE-OFF ready made garments. Which means there can only be one of each special unique piece made just for you. The fabric used in this collection are repurposed from old vintage fabric, vintage tapestry/upholstery or from left over dead-stock fabric.

MADE-TO-ORDER

Our Made-to-order pieces are created by using carefully selected high quality deadstock fabrics. Most items are produced in limited quantities depending on how many meters of a particular fabric that is available to me. Please allow 2-3weeks for production and shipping within the UK. International orders may expect longer delivery times.
